Four-Oh-Four

“Not found”

We gathered here with joy and grace,
But something's gone without a trace.
You clicked with hope, a perfect pair…
Alas! The page just isn't there.

You dreamed of vows and wedding cake,
But now you're met with a mistake.
No ring, no aisle, no doves that soar—
Just sad ol' Error: 404.

Was it cold feet? A runaway link?
A typo made in bridal pink?
The page eloped, it seems, my friend,
And left you scrolling to the end.

But fret not, guest, don't curse your fate,
Love (and links) may just be late.
Try again—or toast instead—
To missing pages and those who wed!
